Our Story

CYBERBEANS was born in 2020, founded by a group of passionate Tesla engineers and designers. We deeply understand Tesla owners' pursuit of quality and innovation, which is why we are dedicated to providing every Tesla owner with the highest quality accessory products.

The "CYBER" in our brand name represents our pursuit of technological innovation, while "BEANS" symbolizes our meticulous attention to every product detail, just like carefully grinding coffee beans. We believe that good accessories are not just functional additions, but enhancements to the driving experience.

From the birth of our first smart cup holder to our current complete product line covering interior, exterior, and functional accessories, CYBERBEANS consistently adheres to the philosophy of "German craftsmanship, Chinese intelligence," contributing our strength to the Tesla ecosystem.
